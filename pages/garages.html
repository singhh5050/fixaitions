Brady Moore, 18

Fixated on Garages

"When I was younger,
about eight or nine
years old, I just thought
garages were really
coooool. When we went to
Laughlin from California,
they had a PLETHORA of
garages—four single car
garages lined up, triple
car garages, trailer
garages. My grandma
would take me on walks,
and I was allowed to go
to each garage and touch
it with two fingers. My
little two-finger touch."
